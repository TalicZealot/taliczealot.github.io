Debuffs
Buffs
Khaos Status
!kstatus instant

Inflicts a random status effect between Poison, Curse or Stone. This effect can be avoided with invincibility or immunity.

Khaos Equipment
!kequipment queued

Randomizes Alucard's equipped gear. If the player has a progression item it gets given back to them and put in the inventory.

Khaos Stats
!kstats queued

Randomizes Alucard's stats, HP and Mana. Sums up current stats for stat pool, hp and mana for points pool, then randomly distributes. Can also give or take points.

Khaos Relics
!krelics queued

Randomizes Alucard's relics. It cannot give Alucard Vlad relics, but has a chance of taking them away.

Pandora's Box
!pandora queued

Randomizes relics, stats, gold, eqipment and inventory and respawns bosses. Cannot give vlad relics. Gives back progression items.

Gamble
!gamble instant

Gambles away a percentage of Alucard's gold for a completely random item.

Bankrupt
!bankrupt queued

Alucard loses all gold, equipment and inventory. If the player has a progression item it gets given back to them and put in the inventory.

Weaken
!weaken queued

Alucard's level, stats, hp and mana get halved. Experience is adjusted so that the player can regain their stats.

Respawn Bosses
!respawnbosses instant

All the defeated bosses in the game rise back to life. Boss zone needs to be reloaded for boss to appear.

Honest Gamer
!honest queued

The honorable SotN players don't abuse spells. Mana locked to 5 for a period.

Subweapons Only
!subsonly queued

Can only use subweapons for combat for a period. Alucard is cursed and mana is locked to 5. Gives Alucard Cube of Zoe, a subweapon and 200 hearts.

Cripple
!cripple queued

Not so fast! Alucard is slowed for a period. Affects walk speed, backdash deceleration, wingsmash speed.

Blood Mana
!bloodmana queued

Spells now cost HP instead of mana. If hp gets low spells start taking from the mana pool. Can't kill the player.

Thirst
!thirst queued

Alucard's vampire nature makes him thirst for blood! Alucard gets Dark Metamorphasis effect, but loses hp over time for a period.

Horde
!horde queued

Khaos has awakened! The castle begins to continuously spawn annoying enemies. Triggers after a viable weaker enemy is encountered in the area. Hp cap is higher in the second castle.

Endurance
!endurance instant

Test your might versus the next boss, who spawns a clone of themselves. Waits for the next boss, so it will not activate in the current room if it has a boss. Does not apply to Beelzebub, Galamoth and Dracula.

Vampire
!vampire instant

Alucard gets Dark Metamorphasis and an attack bonus for an extended period.

Light Help
!lighthelp instant

Activates a Potion, Shield Potion or gives Alucard a minor item.

Medium Help
!mediumhelp instant

Activates an Elixir, Manna Prism or gives Alucard a decent item.

Heavy Help
!heavyhelp instant

Gives Alucard a top-tier item or a non-Vlad progression relic.

Battle Orders
!battleorders queued

A glorious warcry echoes, Alucard's HP get's doubled temporarily, mana is refilled, activates a Shield Potion.

Magician
!magician queued

Mana is locked to max for a period. Activates an Int Potion.

Melty Blood
!melty queued

The hitboxes of most of Alucard's non-special attacks become S+ tier for a period.

Four Beasts
!fourbeasts queued

For a period Alucard becomes fully invincible and has an attack buff.

Za Warudo
!zawarudo instant

Time has stopped! Feel free to throw a road roller at your enemies.

Haste
!haste queued

Increase movement speed for a period. Affects walk speed and wingsmash speed.
